Story

The Dark Age has come. Wars, deceases, drought and plague brought people to the brink of dispair. Dire cult has arrived, preaching paradise through sacrifices. Demons (called Chewmans), satisfied with the gold and sacrifices, are to stop all calamities and suffering, and end the Dark Age. Believers built Unholy Mazes thoughout the country, places where demons are summoned and sacrifices are made. But the is light in this Dark Age. The Ancient Holy Orden, uniting knights, paladins and priesthood, stood against the Dire cult. They swear to clean the land from the demonic creatures and their followers, believing that demons are only bringing more disasters and destroying the world.

How to Play

Game is based on classic arcade games. You play as a Chewman, ancient demon, summoned to the Maze. Main goal is to collect all coins or gems present on each level. You control Chewman by swiping left, right, up or down. To reach the goal you must avoid all enemies and traps on the level and find the way to collect all treasure, as some them may be hidden in places unreachable by simple means. Each collected coin gives you 10 points. When all coins are collected you will proceed to the next level. Stars are awarded for either fast or no-death completion. 3 stars are awarded if player finishes the level without loosing any lives or by completing it within the time limit.

Power-Ups

You can collect power-ups by eating them. Once collected, the power-up activates immediately and causes an effect for some time.

Star: Your enemies are weakened (and start blinking green). While this power-up is active you can eat your enemies instead of them killing you. Each eaten enemy gives you 10 points.

Frost: Your enemies are frozen and can't move, so you have some time to run around them.

Jackhammer: The walls are pulled down for some time so you can get to unreachable places.

Speed: You get some additional speed to move around the map.

Steel Jaws: You are able to eat and permanently destroy the walls.

Bomb: When you collect this power-up, it blows up destroying everything around you, including enemies and walls.

Life: This power-up gives you additional 2 lives to spend trying to reach the final level.

Enemies

Only peoples with strong heart can get into the Maze and try to stop demons from getting to the real world. You must avoid them by all means, as if they catch you, you lose one of your lives. If all lives are lost you lose the game and have to start again.

Nun: Nuns of Ancient Order is most common and weakest enemy, but as they numbers are high, you must beware of them.

Angel: Angels summoned by nuns of Ancient Order can fly on top of walls and other obstacles, making harder to get away from them.

Sorceress: She knows the secrets of powerful dark magic, so be careful to avoid her fireballs and frostbolts. Also she can teleport herself to any place of the level and is immune to the effects of Frost Power-Ups. All those abilities make her very dangerous enemy.

Knight: Trained by the Ancient Order, he is the strongest of your enemies, and will follow you with only intention to kill you with his mighty sword. His armor will kept him from your teeth, defying effects of the Star Power-Ups. Knight can take all the money around him, making them disappear from the Maze.

Rival Chewman: He is one your kind, so he follows the same goal - to collect most of the coins present on the level and he will be glad to remove you from his path to the world domination Also rival chewman can collect power-ups, slowing you down or weakening the enemies. When affected by fire, frost or Star Power-Up, rival trashman change color and became vulnerable to your teeth.

Other obstacles

Enemies are not the only danger in the Maze, so you must be aware of other thing that can kill you in the course of world domination.

Water, lava and acid: You can be drowned, burned or melted, but no matter in what liquid you fall you will lose one of your lives.

Fire Gargoyle: Ancient guardians of the Maze, they breathe fire, that can kill you if you will be in it's way.

Frost Gargoyle: Frost rays from their mouths can significantly reduce your speed, making you much easier target for your enemies.

Teleport: It can transport you to another teleport of same color, but be aware that no one knows what is waiting for you on the other side.
